# Customs & Trade — 60-Second Promo Video Production Kit

**Date**: 2026-05-02
**Owner**: Santosh Ray (founder) → Xentovia video team
**Audience**: B2B — customs brokers, freight forwarders, importers/exporters, 3PLs
**Length**: 60 seconds
**Format**: 16:9 desktop (1920×1080)
**Distribution**: Homepage hero, LinkedIn, YouTube pre-roll, sales decks
**Sound**: Sound-on primary; always-on captions for muted feeds
**Structure**: Pain → Promise → Proof
**Presenter**: Xentovia team member (brand-voice spokesperson)

---

## 1. Script

### Final spoken script (123 words, ~58s at natural pace)

> **[BEAT 1 — Hook, 0:00–0:10]**
> If you've ever filed a customs entry, you know the pain.
> Hours of paperwork. Sanctions checks.
> Holds you never saw coming.
>
> **[BEAT 2 — Pain specifics, 0:10–0:25]**
> A single shipment runs through invoices, BOLs, packing lists, certificates of origin.
> Your team re-keys fifty fields per entry —
> and one mismatch triggers a seven-day hold.
>
> **[BEAT 3 — Solution, 0:25–0:45]**
> Xentovia changes that.
> Our agentic AI reads every shipping document — in any language, any format —
> classifies HS codes, screens parties against OFAC, EAR, and dual-use lists,
> and drafts the entry.
> Your team reviews only the edge cases.
>
> **[BEAT 4 — Outcomes, 0:45–0:55]**
> Filings in fifteen minutes.
> Eighty percent less manual entry.
> Customs holds — eliminated.
>
> **[BEAT 5 — CTA, 0:55–1:00]**
> Xentovia. Customs entries, production-ready.

### Delivery notes for presenter

| Beat | Pace | Tone | Pause / cue |
|------|------|------|-------------|
| Hook | Conversational, slow | Empathetic, knowing | Half-beat after "the pain" |
| Pain specifics | Clipped, list rhythm | Matter-of-fact, slightly urgent | Hard pause before "and one mismatch" |
| Solution | Confident, slightly faster | Authoritative, clear | Quarter-beat between capability phrases |
| Outcomes | Punchy, declarative | Triumphant but measured | Full beat before "eliminated" |
| CTA | Slow, deliberate | Brand-confident, warm | Slight smile on "production-ready" |

**Articulation flags**: enunciate "OFAC", "E-A-R", "BOLs" (say "B-O-Ls"). Do **not** rush HS codes/sanctions list — these are the credibility moment.

## 2. Shot list & AI background prompts

Render each background as a 12–25s loop at 1920×1080, 24fps. Tools: Runway Gen-3 / Sora / Midjourney + Runway video. Match the Xentovia palette: deep navy (#03050a), indigo (#6366f1), purple (#7c3aed), cyan (#06b6d4), with cream text accents.

### Shot 1 — Chaos / Pain (0:00–0:10)

**Mood**: Heavy, dim, unresolved. Presenter framed against motion.
**Primary prompt**:
> *"Cinematic slow-motion shot of a busy customs port at dusk, stacks of shipping containers in cool blue-indigo light, customs paperwork — invoices and bills of lading — drifting through the air in slow motion, dim industrial atmosphere, dark navy and amber color grading, shallow depth of field, photorealistic, subtle film grain, 16:9, 24fps."*

**Fallback prompt** (more abstract):
> *"Aerial top-down shot of stacked customs paperwork piling up on a dark desk, single overhead lamp creating a warm pool of light, papers slowly accumulating, deep indigo background with teal rim, photorealistic, slow zoom out, 16:9."*

### Shot 2 — Specific pain (0:10–0:25)

**Mood**: Tactile, claustrophobic. Documents and red HOLD stamps.
**Primary prompt**:
> *"Macro close-up of customs forms being stamped HOLD in red ink, multiple stamps in rapid succession, papers piling up, single dramatic side-light, deep indigo and red color palette, dust particles in the air, photorealistic, slow camera push-in, 16:9."*

**Fallback prompt**:
> *"Macro shot of multiple shipping documents — commercial invoice, bill of lading, packing list, certificate of origin — fanned out on a dark wooden desk, fingers re-typing values into a glowing computer screen, indigo monitor glow on hands, cinematic, shallow depth, 16:9."*

### Shot 3 — Solution / Pipeline (0:25–0:45)

**Mood**: Futuristic, confident, fluid. The visual turning point.
**Primary prompt**:
> *"Sleek motion graphics animation: six glowing nodes connected by flowing data streams left to right, labeled stages igniting in sequence — Ingest, Extract, Classify, Screen, Review, File — abstract document icons traveling through each node, dark space background with starfield, indigo-to-cyan gradient lighting, soft glow particles, futuristic UI aesthetic, smooth camera dolly, 16:9, 24fps."*

**Fallback prompt**:
> *"Holographic 3D pipeline visualization floating against a dark navy void, translucent panels showing customs documents being scanned by AI, HS codes auto-populating, sanctions database lookups represented as cyan light pulses, premium tech aesthetic with indigo and cyan accents, slow rotating camera, 16:9."*

### Shot 4 — Outcomes (0:45–0:55)

**Mood**: Triumphant, global, smooth.
**Primary prompt**:
> *"Cinematic shot of a stylized world map with global shipping lanes illuminated in cyan and indigo, container ships moving smoothly along the lanes, 'CLEARED' stamps appearing in succession over major ports (Long Beach, Rotterdam, Singapore, Felixstowe), holographic overlay aesthetic, dark navy background, premium tech style, 16:9."*

**Fallback prompt**:
> *"A large countdown clock dissolving from 04:00:00 to 00:15:00, surrounded by floating shipping documents marked CLEARED in cyan, dark space background, soft glow, premium minimalist aesthetic, 16:9."*

### Shot 5 — Brand close (0:55–1:00)

**Mood**: Calm, premium, branded.
**Primary prompt**:
> *"Minimalist dark background with subtle particle starfield, single slowly rotating gradient orb in indigo, purple, and cyan (matching Xentovia brand), centered composition leaving negative space for logo, soft glow, premium brand reveal aesthetic, 16:9, loops smoothly."*

## 4. Recording guide (for the video team)

### Pre-shoot prep

- [ ] **Talent**: solid mid-tone wardrobe, no logos/patterns, no shiny jewelry. Charcoal, navy, deep teal, or warm white all work; **avoid** pure white (clipping) and pure black (loses depth against dark backgrounds).
- [ ] **Makeup**: matte finish — the AI backgrounds are dark, so any face shine will read.
- [ ] **Script rehearsal**: 3 full read-throughs minimum. Time each beat to confirm pacing fits the durations. Mark breath points.
- [ ] **Pronunciation check**: OFAC ("oh-fak"), EAR ("E-A-R" letters), BOL ("B-O-L"), HS code ("H-S code"), ITAR ("eye-tar").

### Camera setup

| Item | Spec |
|------|------|
| Resolution | 4K (3840×2160), down-rezzed to 1080p in edit for sharpness |
| Frame rate | 24fps (cinematic) |
| Codec | ProRes 422 HQ or BRAW |
| Lens | 50mm equiv. for medium close-up, eye-line height |
| Framing | **Two coverage angles per beat**: (a) **Medium shot** — head to waist, more BG visible, leaves room for big stat overlays; (b) **Medium close-up** — top of head to mid-chest, intimate, used on hook and CTA. Talent slightly camera-left in both, leaving space for text overlays camera-right |
| Eyeline | Lens (talent looks straight at viewer) |
| Background | **Green screen** (chroma key) — the AI backgrounds replace the BG in post |

### Lighting (for green-screen compositing)

- **Key light**: soft, 45° camera-left, slightly above eye level
- **Fill**: weaker soft fill, camera-right (around half the key intensity)
- **Backlight / rim**: cool-toned (5600K with gel pushed slightly cyan) — sells the integration with the dark indigo/cyan AI backgrounds
- **Green screen lights**: 2 separate lights on the screen, even illumination, **no spill** on talent

### Audio

- **Mic**: lavalier (Sennheiser MKE 2 / DPA 4060 / equivalent), tucked under collar, ~6" from mouth
- **Backup**: shotgun overhead, just out of frame
- **Sample rate**: 48kHz, 24-bit
- **Levels**: peak -12dB, average -18dB
- **Room**: dampened, no HVAC, phones off, ideally a soft-furnished room or treated booth
- **Capture room tone**: 30s of silence at the location for noise-floor matching in edit

### Performance direction

- **Take a wide and a tight take of every beat** — gives the editor cutaway flexibility
- **3 versions of each beat**: (1) as scripted, (2) slightly faster/punchier, (3) slightly warmer/slower. Pick best in edit
- **Eyes**: directly into lens. No glancing at teleprompter except for HS code / sanctions list mention if needed
- **Hands**: out of frame unless a deliberate gesture is used at "Xentovia changes that" (one open-palm gesture allowed there)
- **Smile cue**: warm half-smile on "production-ready" closing line — not a grin

### Deliverables from the shoot

- [ ] Master takes per beat (wide + tight)
- [ ] Selects file noting preferred takes
- [ ] Room tone file
- [ ] Color chart frame (X-Rite ColorChecker) at start of shoot for grading
- [ ] Slate / clapper at start of every take

## 5. Editing & post guide

### Timeline structure (60.0s total)

| Time | Layer | Content |
|------|-------|---------|
| 0:00–0:10 | BG | Shot 1 (chaos port) |
| 0:00–0:10 | Talent | Hook delivery, keyed against BG |
| 0:00–0:10 | Text | Eyebrow "Customs & Trade" |
| 0:10–0:25 | BG | Shot 2 (HOLD stamps) |
| 0:10–0:25 | Talent | Pain specifics |
| 0:11–0:20 | Text | Three pain stats burst |
| 0:25–0:45 | BG | Shot 3 (pipeline animation) |
| 0:25–0:45 | Talent | Solution delivery |
| 0:28–0:42 | Text | Capability tags rolling |
| 0:45–0:55 | BG | Shot 4 (global outcomes) |
| 0:45–0:55 | Talent | Outcome stats |
| 0:46–0:55 | Text | Three big-stat reveals |
| 0:55–0:60 | BG | Shot 5 (brand orb) |
| 0:55–0:60 | Talent | CTA delivery (or fade out earlier — see note) |
| 0:55–0:60 | Logo + URL + CTA | Brand lockup |

**Note on the close**: the cleanest version drops the talent at 0:55 and lets the brand lockup hold for 5 full seconds. If the team prefers the talent in-frame to the very end, frame her smaller (lower-third), with the lockup centered above.

### Compositing

- **Chroma key**: spill suppression strong; refine matte on hair line
- **Light wrap**: pull a 3–5px ambient light wrap from BG so talent integrates (critical for shot 3's cyan glow)
- **Drop shadow**: subtle, behind talent, cool-toned, 30% opacity max
- **Color match**: grade talent slightly cooler than baseline so the indigo/cyan BGs feel cohesive

### Music & SFX

- **Track type**: cinematic-tech, 90 BPM, building intensity. Avoid generic corporate. Reference: Hans Zimmer "Time" (slow-build) crossed with a tech-product feel
- **Stem options** (pick one and license):
  - Artlist: "Solid Ground" by Yehezkel Raz
  - Musicbed: "Foundations" by Hill (low-key tech anthem)
  - Epidemic: "Distant Lands" by Yehezkel Raz
- **Mix**: music ducks 8–10dB under talent; rises in the gaps between beats; full level on the brand close
- **SFX**:
  - 0:11, 0:14, 0:17 — sharp "stat hit" whoosh + low impact (e.g., Storyblocks "ui_stat_impact_03")
  - 0:25 — gentle whoosh transition into solution
  - 0:46, 0:49, 0:52 — three rising tonal hits, ascending
  - 0:55 — clean cinematic riser into brand close
  - 0:56 — soft logo "shimmer" sound at lockup

### Captions

- Burned-in (always-visible) captions for muted-feed playback
- Style: Inter 600, 28pt, white on 70% black rounded pill, max 2 lines, bottom-center
- Generate from script, then hand-correct technical terms (OFAC, EAR, BOLs, HS codes, dual-use)
- Sync to syllable, not just word

### Color grade

- **LUT base**: gentle teal-orange film LUT
- **Lift shadows**: slight indigo (#1C2742) shadow tint
- **Highlight tint**: subtle cyan
- **Saturation**: -10% global, then +20% on indigo/cyan range only
- **Grain**: 35mm fine grain at 5% opacity for cinematic finish

### Logo / brand close framing

- Center the favicon/logo lockup vertically and horizontally
- Below logo (24px gap): URL `xentovia.ai/solutions/customs-trade` in Inter mono 18pt
- 12px below URL: `Book a Demo →` pill button (indigo→purple gradient, 16pt, white text)
- Hold for 4 full seconds before final fade-to-black

### Export specs

| Variant | Dimensions | Codec | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|-------|
| Master (homepage hero) | 1920×1080, 24fps | H.264, 20Mbps, AAC 320kbps | Burned captions ON |
| LinkedIn organic | 1920×1080 | H.264, 10Mbps | Burned captions ON, file size <200MB |
| YouTube pre-roll (sound-on) | 1920×1080 | H.264, 16Mbps | Captions OFF (rely on YT cc) |
| Square (LinkedIn feed) | 1080×1080, recropped | H.264, 10Mbps | Burned captions ON, recompose talent center |
| Vertical (Story / Reels — optional) | 1080×1920 | H.264, 10Mbps | Recompose talent center, stack overlays |

### Final QC checklist

- [ ] Total runtime ≤ 60.5s
- [ ] Captions sync within ±100ms
- [ ] No keying artifacts at hairline (especially shot 3)
- [ ] Audio peaks ≤ -3dB, dialogue average -16 to -14 LUFS
- [ ] All 7 stat overlays land on-beat with music hits
- [ ] Logo + URL + CTA all readable on a 13" laptop screen at standard viewing distance
- [ ] One muted playthrough — story still makes sense from captions + visuals alone
- [ ] One sound-on playthrough — pacing feels right, no dead space
