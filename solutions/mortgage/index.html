The Problem

Loan files are growing. Manual processing isn't.

Mortgage origination involves hundreds of pages per file — income docs, tax returns, bank statements, disclosures — each requiring extraction, verification, and compliance checks. The industry knows it needs AI, but legacy OCR tools don't cut it.

The Solution

Agentic AI built for mortgage documents.

Xentovia's AI agents process the full mortgage document stack — from 1003 applications to bank statements to closing disclosures. Vision-Language Models understand complex financial documents, while agentic validation catches errors before they reach underwriting.

Intelligent Document Intake

Accept loan files via any channel — borrower portal, LOS upload, email, or batch. Our agents handle PDFs, images, multi-page scans, and e-signed documents.

Auto-Classification

Automatically sort and label every page in a loan file — identifying 1003s, W-2s, tax returns, bank statements, and 30+ other document types instantly.

VLM-Powered Extraction

Vision-Language Models extract borrower data, income figures, asset totals, and employment details from complex financial documents — including multi-column tables and handwritten fields.

Cross-Document Validation

AI agents cross-reference data across documents — matching income on W-2s to tax returns, verifying employer details, and flagging discrepancies automatically.

Human-in-the-Loop QC

Processors review only flagged items with pre-populated fields and side-by-side document views. What took 45 minutes per file now takes 10.

LOS Delivery

Validated, structured data flows directly into your Loan Origination System — indexed, categorized, and ready for underwriting review.

Document Types

Bank Statements
