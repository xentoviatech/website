The Solution

An agentic AI pipeline built for insurance.

Xentovia's AI agents process insurance documents end-to-end — from the moment a claim is filed to the moment structured data lands in your claims management system. With human-in-the-loop verification for every decision that matters.

Document Intake

Accept claims documents from any channel — email, portal uploads, fax, mobile photos. Our agents handle PDFs, images, scanned documents, and even handwritten forms.

Smart Classification

AI agents automatically classify incoming documents by type — FNOL, medical records, police reports, repair estimates — and route them to the correct processing pipeline.

VLM-Based Extraction

Vision-Language Models read documents the way a human would — understanding layout, tables, handwriting, and context to extract structured data with high accuracy.

Agentic Validation

Extraction results are validated through multi-agent pipelines — cross-referencing fields, checking policy limits, verifying dates, and flagging inconsistencies automatically.

Human-in-the-Loop Review

Low-confidence extractions and edge cases are routed to human reviewers with pre-populated fields and highlighted areas of concern, reducing review time by 60%.

System Delivery

Validated, structured data is delivered directly to your claims management system via API — no manual re-entry, no copy-paste, no lag.
