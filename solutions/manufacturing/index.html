As production speeds increase and quality standards tighten, human visual inspection becomes the bottleneck. Fatigue, inconsistency, and throughput limitations mean defects slip through — leading to recalls, waste, and customer dissatisfaction.

Cost of Escaped Defects

Defects caught at the customer are 10-100x more expensive than those caught on the line. Recalls, warranty claims, and reputation damage compound the cost.

The Solution

Visual AI agents for production-grade quality.

Xentovia's Visual AI agents combine computer vision, anomaly detection, and agentic decision-making to inspect products at line speed. Every defect is classified, graded, and routed — with human-in-the-loop review for critical decisions.

Surface Defect Detection

Detect scratches, dents, cracks, stains, and surface irregularities on any material — metal, plastic, glass, fabric, or composite. Sub-millimeter precision at production speed.

Dimensional Measurement

Vision-based dimensional verification ensures parts meet tolerance specifications. Compare against CAD models or reference standards in real time.

Packaging Verification

Verify labels, barcodes, seal integrity, fill levels, and packaging completeness. Catch missing items, misaligned labels, and tamper indicators before shipping.

Assembly Validation

Verify that assembled products have all components in the correct position and orientation. Detect missing parts, reversed components, and incorrect assemblies.

Quality Grading

Automatically grade products into quality tiers based on visual characteristics. Consistent, objective grading that eliminates inspector subjectivity.

Real-Time Alerting

Instant alerts when defect rates spike, process drift is detected, or quality thresholds are breached. Stop problems before they become line shutdowns.

How It Works

From camera to quality decision in milliseconds.

1

Capture

Production line cameras capture images of every unit at inspection points.

2

Classify

AI classifies each image — identifying product type, orientation, and inspection region.

3

Detect

Anomaly detection and defect classification models identify issues at sub-pixel precision.

4

Grade

Products are graded by severity — pass, rework, or reject — based on your quality criteria.

5

Act

Alerts fire, reports generate, and line controls trigger — all in real time.
